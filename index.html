Tips & Specifics
The things that actually move the needle
📈
Progressive overload — gradually add weight or reps each week. This is the #1 driver of muscle growth.
🥩
Protein intake — aim for ~0.8–1g per pound of bodyweight. Spread evenly: 20–30g per meal.
🔁
Consistency is queen — don't skip lower body + full body days. Those are your glute builders.
🥇
Protein first rule — eat your protein source first at meals so you don't fill up on carbs.
🍚
Pair carbs + protein — makes meals more satisfying and helps muscles recover (rice + chicken, toast + eggs).
🍿
Upgrade your snacks — love snacking? Just pick higher-protein versions (popcorn + jerky, chips + cottage cheese dip).
💡
Don't overcomplicate it — one protein shake + eggs at breakfast bumps your daily intake by 30–40g easily.
🍗
Batch cook once a week — grill or bake a pack of chicken/turkey to add to meals all week long.
